Vamana Gullu

A classic board game with turn-based sowing and captures.

How to Play

  • Each pit starts with 5 seeds, and your side is the bottom row.
  • On your turn, click any non-empty pit on your side to sow the seeds counter-clockwise.
  • If the last seed lands in a pit that already has seeds, the sowing continues from there.
  • If the last seed lands in an empty pit on your side, you capture the seeds from the opposite pit on the CPU side.
  • The game ends when one row is empty; the player with the most captured seeds wins.
